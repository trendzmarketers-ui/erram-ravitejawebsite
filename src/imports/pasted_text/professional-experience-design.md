Professional Experience Page – Design Instructions
Overall Page Style

Create a clean, premium light theme page with lots of whitespace and strong typography hierarchy.

Design inspiration:

Apple leadership pages

Stripe team profiles

Modern startup founder portfolios

Architecture studio websites

Page should feel editorial, elegant, and professional.

Page Structure

The page should contain 5 main sections.

1️⃣ Page Header
2️⃣ Experience Timeline
3️⃣ Key Achievements
4️⃣ Skills & Expertise
5️⃣ Education

1. Page Header (Top Section)

Large clean introduction.

Layout: Centered

Title

Professional Experience

Subtitle

A journey across digital marketing, creative design, business strategy, and operational innovation.

Optional small text

10+ brands transformed
60+ digital campaigns executed
30+ digital platforms developed

Typography:
Large editorial heading.

2. Experience Timeline

Create a vertical timeline layout for work history.

Left side:
Timeline line with dots.

Right side:
Experience cards.

Each card should contain:

Company Name

Role
Duration
Location

Short description.

Example layout:

TrendZ Marketers
Co-Founder
2023 – Present | Hyderabad

Description text.

Design style:

White cards

Soft shadow

Rounded corners

Minimal border

Spacing should be generous and breathable.

3. Key Achievements Section

Use a 4-column statistic grid.

Example:

Design style:
Large numbers
Minimal text
Subtle animation when scrolling.

Interaction & Animation

Include subtle premium animations:

• Cards fade in on scroll
• Timeline dots animate while scrolling
• Hover effect on experience cards
• Numbers count-up animation in achievements

• a founder profile
• a creative strategist portfolio
• a premium leadership bio page

Not like a traditional resume.
